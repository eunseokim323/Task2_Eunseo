Conduct Risk Factor Analysis: Assess areas that have potential fall hazards to identify risks.
Eliminate Risk Factors: Choose safe construction methods and work practices to mitigate these risks.
Implement Fall Prevention Systems: Plan to install systems like safety railings to restrict access to dangerous areas.
Install Fall Protection Measures: Equip fall-prone areas with safety nets or belts for worker protection.
Ensure Suitable Fall Prevention Measures: Match horizontal and vertical fall prevention strategies to specific site hazards and conduct professional analysis to secure anchor points.
Plan for Emergency Rescue Facilities: Prepare facilities and equipment for rescuing workers in the event of a fall.
Establish Comprehensive Training: Develop a thorough training program focused on fall prevention and rescue techniques.
Document the Fall Prevention Plan: Record all the steps in a comprehensive fall prevention plan.
