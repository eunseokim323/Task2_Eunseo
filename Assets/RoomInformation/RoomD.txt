Room D Information
Install Lifting Lugs or Eyebolts: When lifting, use lifting lugs or eyebolts for safer operation.
Install Correct Anti-Overshoot Devices: Ensure the right anti-overshoot devices are in place.
Safe Work Inside Aerial Platforms: Perform tasks safely within the working space of aerial work platforms.
Prohibit Unsafe Cargo Loading: Avoid unsafe cargo loading and ensure visibility when stacking items.
Prohibit Driving with Suspended Cargo on Forks: Do not drive or make sharp turns while cargo is suspended on forklift forks.
Assign a Supervisor for Crane Operations: Have a designated supervisor on site during crane operations.
Prohibit Underneath Access During Rigging Operations: Do not allow access beneath the area during rigging operations.
