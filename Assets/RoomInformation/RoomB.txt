Room B Information
Organize Materials at Height: Ensure materials placed at high locations are well organized and secured.
Avoid Unsafe Placement: Do not store materials on external scaffolding or near openings where they could easily fall.
Install Safety Nets: Set up nets and other facilities designed to catch falling objects, preventing them from reaching lower levels.
Secure Nets Between Structures: Install falling object prevention nets between scaffolding and adjacent walls for additional safety.
Maintain Prevention Nets: Regularly maintain falling object prevention nets and adhere to prescribed maintenance guidelines.
Restrict Access Near Heavy Machinery: Prohibit workers from entering areas where heavy lifting equipment, like cranes, are in operation.
Inspect Lifting Equipment: Regularly check the ropes and binding methods used in lifting equipment to ensure they are secure and reliable.
